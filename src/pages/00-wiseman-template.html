The Return

Wisemans Surf Lodge presents an opportunity for asset ownership, a return profile and private usage. An investment trust has been created and we anticipate that there will be a Product Disclosures Statement released in the first half of 2021. The Wisemans Surf Lodge will be operated by BALNCE and will earn revenues from annual memberships, corporate and private event sales, food and beverage and any ancillary revenues. From its revenue generation, BALNCE will pay rent to the investment trust, generating an annual yield for investors.

If you’re interested in taking up this special investment opportunity, let’s chat.

Technology

Partnering with SurfLoch, our wave technology will break new water and peel away from the systems used at Surf Lakes, URBNSURF and Kelly Slater Wave Company. It utilises individual chambers, known as caissons, to propel water through concentrated volumes of compressed air. With individual control of each caisson, the technology can produce waves every 17 seconds of up to 2.5 metres and with unparalleled wave customisation.

“I’ve used other pneumatic technologies out there, and if you liken their variety to playing different notes on a keyboard, SurfLoch technology is like playing a theatre organ”

CHEYNE MAGNUSSEN / CHIEF
HYDRO OFFICER (PSSC)

The design

A leading Australian architectural mind, Kelvin Ho’s honest and relaxed design was a most welcome addition to the team at Wisemans Surf Lodge. From Merivale hotspots to Bassike and Dion Lee, his creative eye has been responsible for some of Sydney’s most recognisable spaces and is now fixed on bringing to life a playful boutique resort with minimal environmental impact.

“Australia’s first resort-style surfing destination, Wisemans Surf Lodge boasts a wave pool roughly the size of four football fields stacked. If that doesn’t get your primed for an Aussie summer, maybe the 9-hole golf course, 54 hotel suites, restaurant, bar and riverfront access might”

CHEYNE MAGNUSSEN / CHIEF
