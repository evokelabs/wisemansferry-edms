Just 60 minutes from North Sydney or 45 minutes up the river from Palm Beach is Wisemans Ferry. Nestled on the fringes of the breathtaking Dharug National Park in a valley of ancient sandstone, this pristine patch of bushland will soon be home to Australia’s only surf lodge.

Formally known as the Retreat at Wisemans, this existing 45 acre site will be revitalised by Sydney architect, Kelvin Ho from Akin Atelier.

Wisemans Surf Lodge will introduce a unique facility for surfing, golfing, wellness, dining, and events, plus activities for the kids. All of which make this facility a first of its kind.
