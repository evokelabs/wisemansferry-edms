Thank you for submitting your application to invest in the Wisemans Surf Lodge Property Trust. We will be in touch within the next 15 business days to update you on your application status. Please add us as a known email address so you don’t miss any important future updates. Here’s the remaining stages you can expect to complete your investment:

1. Application verification

We verify the information you have supplied and process your application.

2. Further information, if required

Depending on your investment vehicle, the investment manager may require further information such as a certified trust excerpt to complete your application, but our team will be in touch within the application verification process if necessary.

3. Investment payment

Once your application is approved, we will provide you with your unique Customer Reference Number and details on how to make your payment via BPAY to the trust account.

4. Investment confirmation

Once payment is cleared you will receive a unit holder statement, officially recognising you as an investor in Australia’s first wave pool resort property.

Once your investment is complete you’ll receive communications from the operator, BALNCE, regarding your membership offer and from Registry Direct to set-up portal access for your investment administration in the Wisemans Surf Lodge Property Trust. In the meantime, if you have any questions, please do not hesitate to book a call with our Investment team.
