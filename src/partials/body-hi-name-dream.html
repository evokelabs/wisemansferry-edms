If you dream of empty lineups and that feeling of freedom you only get on a surf trip, you’re not alone. Hungry for perfect A-frames, we went searching for a solution to crowded waves – something that would redefine the Sydney surf experience.

Wisemans Surf Lodge will make world-class waves under orange cliffs and white gum eucalypts. With a 9-hole golf course, member clubhouse, restaurant and bar, the 54-suite lodge will create a new surf community in a patch of bush rich with convict and indigenous history.
